Reason for Inspection: Routine

General Comments: A routine inspection was conducted at the site location of 400 W Georgia, with Young Sook Lee. Observed frozen beef stored on top of two inserts with shredded chicken and beef. Lack of/Inadequate Food Safety Plan

Food safety plan must be on-site for review of all potentially hazardous food items served in this unit. No temperature monitoring occuring of the cooler. Temperature logs were provided to the operator.

Food Not Protected From Contamination - Microbiological

Observed raw meats stored above ready to eat foods, such as vegetables. This is direct cross-contamination.

Ensure all raw meats are stored on lowest shelf in coolers, separated from other foods in order to prevent potential contamination by dripping.

 1 0

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

Observed an insert of raw, frozen beef, cooked, shredded beef and shredded chicken on the grill. Products were tested at 4-6 C.

All potentially hazardous food items must be stored at 4 C and below, within the cooler OR cook the food item to a minimum of 74 C and maintain hot at 60 C and greater. The frozen beef was removed and stored in the cooler. If foods are stored at room temperature, products will be removed and discarded.

 1 0

Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

The condiments, such as cut onions, tomatoes and kim chi was stored in a container, on top of ice packs. This is not good storage practices. Food products must be fully submerged into the ice and achieve a minimum internal temperature of 4 C and below. Ice packs were placed around the three inserts in order to maintain 4 C and below.

 1 0

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

Bleach water solution must be provided to the facility. Use the following steps: 1. use a minimum of ï¿½ teaspoon of bleach to 1 L of water OR 1 ounce of bleach to 1 gallon of water 2. use a container and immerse all dishcloths into this solution OR use a spray bottle 3. use throughout the daytime and change this solution every 2-3 hours.

No bleach sanitizer was found on-site for sanitizing purposes. All surfaces, utensils and equipment must be sanitized with a minimum of 100 ppm of bleach.

 1 0

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

Operator was informed that the commissary is used for the warewashing of dishes. However, for emergencies, this facility must be equipped to wash, rinse and sanitize on-site. Bleach is required for this purpose. Do not use a wool pad to wash dishes.

Ensure all dishes and utensils are cleaned and sanitized using the following method: 1) Prescrape all dishes 2) Sink 1: Hot soapy water wash 3) Hot water rinse under running water 4) Sink 2: Hot water and sanitizer (1/2 teaspoon of bleach to 1 L of water OR 1 ounce of bleach to 1 gallon of water) 5) Racks: Air dry

Lack of/Inadequate Sanitation Plan

No sanitation plan was on-site for verification purposes. Maintain a copy for the facility and for verification purposes.

Improper Sanitary Facilities

Operator had started preparing foods, however the handsink was not supplied with hot water. For appropriate handwashing, the operator must ensure the hand sink is fully supplied with hot water, prior to any type of processing.

Use the following correct handwashing technique: 1. Wet hands with lukewarm water, 2. Apply liquid soap, 3. Lather for 30 seconds, 4. Rinse, 5. Dry using paper towel. Use towel to turn off taps.
