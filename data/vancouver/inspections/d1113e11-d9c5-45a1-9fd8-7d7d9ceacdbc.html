Inadequate Cooling and/or Refrigerated Storage of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

The preparation cooler was recording at 6-7 C. Foods were piled very high in each insert.

Use the following recommendations in order to maintain effective temperature control. Tips: 1. Do not stack inserts. 2. Use metal inserts for potentially hazardous foods. 3. Use probe thermometers for monitoring food temperatures. 4. Keep large lid closed as much as possible, and cover potentially hazardous foods with individual lids. 5. Service the cooler so that the bottom portion is storing food as close to 0C without freezing. 6. Do not overfill inserts.

Inadequate Hot Holding of Potentially Hazardous Food(s)

The hot holding unit must be operating at 60 C and greater. The food product must be time logged on a white board, as to the type of pizza and timers must be present for each type of pizza. Once 2 hours surpass, product must be removed and discarded immediately. Staff indicated that the pizza is dry after 30 minutes, it is discarded. Timers were located at the hot holding units, ensure that each type of pizza is recorded on the time log.

Improper Cleaning, Sanitizing of Equipment and Utensils

Ensure that bleach sanitizer is used for sanitizing all surfaces, utensils and equipment. Sanitizer must be used each day, prior to any processing. Inadequate Insect/Rodent Control

Remove all droppings. Clean and sanitize areas with a bleach-water sanitizing solution (1 part of bleach to 10 parts of water: 500 ppm). Clean kitchen and keep clean. Ensure all foods are stored in pest proof containers with tight fitting lids. Obtain services of a professional pest control company to eliminate infestation. Rodent proof the building by sealing holes with steel wool and spray foam, and keeping the back door closed at all times. Provide additional mouse traps, including snap traps, glue/ sticky boards, and tin cat (metal box) style traps.

Observed rodent droppings near the hot water tank. Continue to monitor for cockroaches and provide pest control services on a weekly basis.
